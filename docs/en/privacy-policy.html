Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

1. Introduction

At Hairmatic ("we", "our", or "app"), we greatly value our users' privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our application, we may collect the following information:

  • Photos: Photos you upload for hairstyle trials
  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version
  • Usage Data: In-app interactions and preferences
  • Analytics Data: App performance and usage statistics

Important: Photos you upload are used only for AI hairstyle processing and are deleted from our servers after processing is complete.

3. Use of Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing AI-powered hairstyle recommendations and transformations
  • Personalizing and improving the app experience
  • Identifying and resolving technical issues
  • Providing user support
  • Fulfilling our legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Service Providers: Our trusted partners for AI processing and cloud storage
  • Legal Requirements: When necessary to comply with legal processes
  • With Your Consent: Other situations where we have your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Secure data transmission with SSL/TLS encryption
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited personnel access

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary. Processed photos are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours after AI processing is complete.

7. User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request correction of your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to data processing
  • Request data portability

8. Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When significant changes are made, we will notify you through in-app notifications or email.
