Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

Beyza Zengin ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Hairmatic mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

By using Hairmatic, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Photos and Images

When you use our AI hairstyle generation feature, you provide photos from your camera or photo library. These photos are:

  • Processed in real-time using Google's Gemini AI API
  • Transmitted securely via base64 encoding to generate your styled images
  • NOT stored on our servers — we do not retain, save, or have access to your photos
  • NOT used to improve our products — Google's Paid Tier API does not use your data for product improvement (as per Google's API Terms)

The AI-generated hairstyle images are stored only on your device in the App's local gallery.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

Analytics Data

We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data to improve our App. This may include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Session duration and frequency

This data is collected in aggregate form and does not personally identify you.

Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases, we use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions and credits. RevenueCat may collect:

  • Anonymous user identifiers
  • Purchase history and subscription status
  • Transaction data processed by Apple

We do not have access to your payment details (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.) as all payments are processed securely by Apple.

1.3 Device Permissions

The App requires access to:

Permission Purpose
Camera To take photos for hairstyle generation
Photo Library To select existing photos and save generated images

You can revoke these permissions at any time in your device settings.

1.4 Local Storage

We store limited data locally on your device:

  • Keychain: To track free trial usage (persists across reinstalls)
  • UserDefaults: To track onboarding completion status
  • App Storage: To save your generated hairstyle images

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process your photos through AI to generate hairstyle images
  • Manage your subscription and credit balance
  • Analyze App usage to improve user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal obligations

3.1 Third-Party Service Providers

We share data with the following service providers who assist in operating our App:

Service Purpose Data Shared
Google Gemini AI AI image generation Base64 encoded photos (temporarily for processing only)
Firebase Analytics Usage analytics Anonymous usage data
RevenueCat Subscription management Anonymous user ID, purchase data
Apple App Store Payment processing Transaction data (handled by Apple)

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests by public authorities.

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmitted to third-party APIs uses secure HTTPS connections
  • Photos are not stored on any external servers
  • Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple's secure infrastructure

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Photos: Not retained — processed in real-time and immediately discarded after AI generation
  • Generated Images: Stored only on your device until you delete them
  • Analytics Data: Retained according to Firebase's standard retention policies
  • Purchase Data: Retained as required for subscription management and legal compliance
